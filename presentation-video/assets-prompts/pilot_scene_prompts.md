# Pilot Scene Prompts

## Scene 1 - Pilot Context

Realistic documentary video in Abuja, Nigeria. A patient arrives at a selected PHC while staff prepare for a small pilot. Warm daylight, professional public-health tone, no fake government seal, no exaggerated technology.

## Scene 2 - PHC Intake

Nurse Grace captures Amina Yusuf's basic request at Demo PHC Garki. Show simple intake, no private diagnosis, no readable medical record, no emergency.

## Scene 3 - Doctor Review

Dr. Musa Ibrahim reviews the request and speaks remotely where appropriate. The scene should show professional clinical decision support without making AI the focus.

## Scene 4 - Prescription or Referral

Show the doctor creating either a prescription workflow or referral summary. Pharmacist Ada and Mr. Bello see only the information relevant to their role.

## Scene 5 - Ministry Dashboard

Show a Ministry dashboard user viewing aggregate indicators: patient requests, consultations, referrals, prescriptions, follow-up visibility, and response time.

## Scene 6 - Pilot Timeline

Show a practical timeline: alignment, configuration, training, controlled launch, evaluation, and scale decision.

## Scene 7 - Handover

Formal conference room ready for the human presentation. End with the handover to Jonah Baka's live PowerPoint.
