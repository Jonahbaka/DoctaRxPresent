# Character Prompts

Use realistic Nigerian public-health characters. The characters are demonstration actors only. They must not act as Jonah Baka, ask for approval on Jonah's behalf, or imply government endorsement.

## Amina Yusuf - Patient

Nigerian woman in her mid-30s, calm but concerned, using a mobile phone and visiting a primary healthcare centre in Abuja. Respectful documentary tone, no visible diagnosis, no emergency drama.

## Nurse Grace - PHC Officer

Nigerian PHC nurse/officer in a modest but clean clinic environment, entering basic intake information on a tablet. Warm, practical, and professional.

## Dr. Musa Ibrahim - Doctor

Nigerian doctor reviewing a case in a modest consultation room, using a laptop or tablet for remote review. Professional and calm, not dramatic.

## Pharmacist Ada - Pharmacy Partner

Nigerian pharmacist in a community pharmacy setting, reviewing a prescription workflow without showing private patient data.

## Mr. Bello - Referral Officer

Hospital referral officer reviewing a referral summary and follow-up status in a general hospital office.

## Ministry Dashboard User

Senior public-health administrator in a Federal Secretariat style office viewing aggregate pilot indicators only.
